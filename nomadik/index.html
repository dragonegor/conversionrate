FAQ

MEMBERSHIP INFO

Will my gear kit be the same as everyone else?

Everybody could get the same box, but it’s not guaranteed. Our adventure experts work hard to find the latest gear, but many items are only available in limited quantities. But rest assured, the value, quality, and quantity of the items in all variations of our kits are comparable each month.

When will my gear kit ship?

We typically ship by the 24th of each month (except December, which will ship by the 20th). If you become a member prior to the 13th of the month, your Welcome Box will ship by the 24th of that month. If you become a member after the 13th of the month, your Welcome Box will ship by the 24th of the following month.

We understand you may be eager to get your new gear! If that’s the case, we can expedite your first month free of charge. Just email info@thenomadik.com after you subscribe.

PLEASE NOTE: Due to COVID-19, shipping carriers are experiencing intermittent delays nationwide. Shipments could be delayed while in transit.

When will I be charged?

You'll be charged on the day you sign up. After that, you will automatically get charged on your renewal date (varies by monthly plan type).

Can I cancel anytime?

No problem! Of course, we would love to have you as a member, but you can cancel or re-activate whenever you want. You can cancel directly in your customer account, without having to email or call us. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees. If you would like to cancel your membership, you must do so before the first day of the month. For the 1-month plan, you need to cancel on or before the first of each month. For the 6 and 12-month plans, you need to cancel before the end of your membership term.

To cancel a subscription:
1. Login to your account
2. Click the orange UPDATE button under Manage Subscriptions
3. Click the green EDIT button under Your Subscriptions
4. Click the green CANCEL SUBSCRIPTION button
5. Answer the questions and click the green SUBMIT button

Please note that canceling your account does not provide you with a refund for any paid boxes, nor does it stop a pending charge. Your cancellation will go into effect the following billing cycle.

If you cancel after the first of the month, you will still receive your gear kit and will not be eligible for a refund. Your cancellation will be effective the following billing cycle.

If you subscribe and cancel your membership right away, you will receive your first month, and all subsequent months will be canceled (unless you prepaid for a 6 or 12-month membership). 6 and 12-month memberships are prepaid and therefore non-refundable.
